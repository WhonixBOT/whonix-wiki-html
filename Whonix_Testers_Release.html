Stay Tuned![edit]

As Whonix ™ 15 was released on 1 July 2019 and work is ongoing, a testers release of Whonix ™ 16 is not yet available -- check back here at a later date.

Interested readers can refer to the Whonix ™ 16 roadmap [archive] and Development forum [archive] to see where Whonix is heading. Major items include:

  • A Whonix-Host operating system (tickets [archive]); [1]
  • CoyIM secure XMPP chat client (ticket [archive]); and
  • A policy for inclusion of compiled software [archive].
