Introduction[edit]

https://github.com/Whonix/grub-live

One of primary objectives of grub-live is preventing malware from gaining persistence and having an unchanged system after reboot.

It is also recommended to regularly boot into persistent mode for installation of updates.

Installation[edit]

As per Whonix Packages for Debian Hosts.
