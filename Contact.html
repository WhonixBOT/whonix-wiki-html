Private Communications Policy[edit]

Please avoid private communications whenever possible. Whonix ™ is a Freedom Software [archive] project. Free as in Price as well as in Freedom. (Why?) Therefore as many commuications as possible should happen in public for transparency. Post in Whonix ™ forums [archive] whenever appropriate and possible.

Encrypted Communications Policy[edit]

Encrypted communications are accepted in the following categories: Priority Support, Professional Support, for reporting security issues, for private inquiries.

Social Media[edit]

There are a few Whonix ™ Social Media Profiles, but they should not be relied upon for the latest Whonix ™ news. See Stay Tuned for reliable ways of following Whonix ™ news.

In addition, when appropriate Whonix ™ developers should be directly contacted by email and not via social media as these are likely to get unnoticed.

Some users may wish to follow or like the available Whonix ™ profiles (with an anonymous account). This applies if it is safe to talk about using the Whonix ™ platform and you want to share your experience with others. This is also a small way to contribute to the project.

Whonix ™ is Supported by Evolution Host DDoS Protected VPS. Stay private and get your VPS with Bitcoin or Monero.
